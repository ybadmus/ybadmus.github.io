F.A.Q

We understand you may have questions about our mission, children, and how you can help. Here are the answers that matter most.

Where is United Heart Foundation Orphanage located

United Heart Foundation Orphanage is located in Bawjiase, in the Central Region of Ghana. Our home is situated in a peaceful community where children can grow in a safe and supportive environment.

When was the orphanage established

The orphanage was founded in 2007 out of a deep desire to respond to the growing number of vulnerable children in Bawjiase and its surrounding areas. Since then, United Heart Foundation Orphanage has grown into a nurturing home that provides shelter, education, and care for children who have lost parents or come from disadvantaged backgrounds. Our foundation continues to expand its impact every year.

How many children do you currently care for

At present, we care for over 40 children of different ages, from toddlers to teenagers. Each child is unique and comes with their own storyâ€”whether orphaned, abandoned, or from families struggling to provide basic needs. We ensure that every child receives equal love, guidance, and support. Our goal is to meet prepare them for a brighter future.

Can I visit the orphanage in Bawjiase

Yes, visits are very welcome! We encourage donors, sponsors, volunteers, and well-wishers to see firsthand the impact of their support. Visits give you the chance to interact with the children, tour our facilities, and witness the love and care we provide. To ensure safety and proper coordination, we kindly ask that all visits be arranged in advance through our administration office - (+233) 244 809 096.

How is the orphanage funded

United Heart Foundation Orphanage is primarily sustained through donations and sponsorships from individuals, families, organizations, and corporate partners. We also organize fundraising activities and community programs to raise awareness and resources for the children. Every contribution, no matter the size, directly supports the daily running of the home, covering food, school fees, medical expenses, and other essential needs for the children under our care.

What kind of support do the children receive

The children at United Heart Foundation Orphanage receive holistic care. This includes nutritious meals, comfortable shelter, access to healthcare, and quality education. Beyond physical needs, we also provide emotional support, mentoring, and moral guidance.

How can I make a donation

We accept donations through several convenient channels, including bank transfers, mobile money and crypto. In addition to financial support, we also welcome in-kind donations such as food, clothing, books, and school supplies. Every donation, big or small, makes a meaningful impact.

Can I sponsor a child directly

Yes, we offer a child sponsorship program where donors can directly support a childâ€™s growth and development. Sponsorship covers essentials such as education, healthcare, food, and clothing. This one-on-one support ensures that children know they are loved and cared for, while also giving sponsors the joy of seeing lives transformed.

Do you accept volunteers

Absolutely! Volunteers are a vital part of our mission. We welcome both local and international volunteers to contribute their skills in areas such as teaching, caregiving, mentoring, and vocational training. Volunteers not only help meet the daily needs of the children but also bring joy, friendship, and inspiration. By sharing their time and talents, volunteers create lifelong memories while making a positive difference in young lives.

How can I contact United Heart Foundation Orphanage

You can reach us by phone, email, or through our official website contact form. Our team is always ready to answer questions, guide donors, and provide further information about our programs. We also encourage anyone interested in partnerships, sponsorship, or volunteering to get in touch.
