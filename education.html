At United Hearts Foundation International School, we believe education is one of the greatest gifts we can give a child. Many of the children in our care are either orphans or come from low-income families within the surrounding communities. For them, access to quality education is often out of reach due to financial challenges and lack of resources. That is why our school was established — to break down these barriers and ensure that every child has the opportunity to learn, dream, and succeed.


We provide free and subsidized education, making it possible for children who might otherwise never step into a classroom to receive the academic foundation they deserve. Beyond reading, writing, and arithmetic, we focus on nurturing the whole child by encouraging creativity, critical thinking, and moral values that prepare them not just for exams, but for life.


Our dedicated teachers and volunteers work tirelessly to create a supportive learning environment where children feel safe, respected, and inspired to reach their potential. We also integrate mentorship programs, helping students build confidence and guiding them toward discovering their unique talents and passions.


Your support makes this possible. Every donation helps cover tuition, books, uniforms, and other essential resources that give these children the gift of education. Together, we can break the cycle of poverty and create lasting change, one child at a time


Through this school, we are not only shaping young minds but also sowing seeds of hope, opportunity, and lasting transformation. Every child educated today is a step closer to a brighter tomorrow for the entire community.
