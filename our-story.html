Our Story

The vision for United Hearts Foundation was born during weekly prayer meetings led by Pastor Paul. He began to notice a growing number of women attending with children - many of whom had lost one or both parents and were being raised by elderly grandmothers or guardians in extremely difficult conditions. These children faced immense challenges, including lack of access to proper shelter, education, nutrition, and healthcare.

Burdened by compassion and guided by faith, Pastor Paul began supporting these children out of his own modest means, enrolling them in boarding and private schools and providing for their basic needs. In 2007, he formally established the orphanage, starting with just five children from the community.

The early years were challenging—children slept on bare floors, and providing even three meals a day was a constant struggle. But through prayer, perseverance, and the generous support of both local and international donors, the orphanage gradually grew in strength and capacity.
