Access to clean and safe drinking water remains one of the greatest challenges for many families in Awutu Bawjiase and surrounding communities. At United Hearts Foundation, we have established a Clean Water Project that produces and distributes affordable sachet water to meet this vital need.


This initiative does more than provide safe water‚Äîit also creates a sustainable source of income for the orphanage, helping us cover daily operational costs such as food, healthcare, and education for the children in our care. By turning a community challenge into an opportunity, the project improves public health while ensuring long-term financial stability for the foundation.


With your support, we can expand production capacity, improve distribution, and reach even more families with safe drinking water. Every contribution directly strengthens both the health of the community and the future of the children we serve.
